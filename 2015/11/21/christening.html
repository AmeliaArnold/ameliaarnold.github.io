Amelia’s Christening

Nov 21, 2015

Today Amelia was Christened in Crowland Abby in Lincolnshire. This is where we got married just over 5 years ago. It was so nice to have the Christening service in the same place, with the same vicar and many of the same friends and relations in attendance.

Amelia was very well behaved throughout the service; indeed, she slept through most of it. When it came to the actual Christening part we were concerned that the vicar’s lapel microphone would pick up her snoring!

After the service we had a lunch at St Nicholas House.

Many thanks to everyone who attended, especially Amelia’s Godparents; Jason, Dom, Shelly and Michaela.
